What the current site doesn't do

The story isn't being told. The reviews aren't being shown. The product range isn't being merchandised. The brand reads as a generic Shopify store, not a 40-year category leader. How we'll build it
Three principles.
Every tier. No exceptions.
01 / Conversion
Built to sell, not just to look good
Every page decision starts with "does this move the buyer forward?" Sticky add-to-cart, trust strip, urgency cues, post-purchase upsells, abandoned cart logic. Beautiful is the floor. Converting is the ceiling.
02 / SEO-aligned
The rebuild protects the SEO investment
We build with schema baked into the templates, clean URL structure, semantic HTML, and Core Web Vitals as a release criterion. Every line of code complements the SEO roadmap rather than working against it.
03 / Brand-first
A storefront that matches the story
40 years of heritage, family ownership, and proprietary process get built into the experience — not hidden in a forgotten "About" page. The site finally feels like the category leader it actually is.

Why this matters now
SEO and the rebuild,
working in lockstep.
The opportunity
Build the rebuild on top of the SEO work — not after it
The SEO roadmap currently in motion will fix the technical issues, implement schema, and start moving impressions back up. If the site is rebuilt afterwards on a different foundation, much of that work has to be redone. Coordinating the rebuild with the SEO timeline means schema, redirect logic, URL structure, and content strategy are all baked into the rebuild from day one — and the SEO investment compounds rather than resets.
The mechanics
One source of truth for technical decisions
We coordinate directly with the SEO partner on every technical decision: URL structure, schema implementation, redirect maps, site architecture, and the blog infrastructure where Luke's content strategy actually lives. The result is a single coherent system rather than two parallel workstreams stepping on each other. The brand gets one site, built once, optimised for everything that matters.
